Welcome to the best Online Advisement Platform.

Student
Students can create their own profile.
Students will be able to see their profile with basic information such as their EMPLID, year, school, GPA, and edit to add more content such as bio.
Students can track their degree completion.
The checklist, showcases your progress within each requirement needed to graduate. Only students who have reached all the requirements can then press to apply for graduation.
Students have automated advisement.
The live advisement form has filled in the grades of all the courses the student has taken, and includes classes they are currently taking. Allows Students to choose courses they intend on taking the following semester, and automatically submit to advisor(s).
Can view status of workflow
The students can also view their workflow. This Status tab displays approval/steps of automated advising and notifies when a student is eligible for enrollment. Students have two viewing options to see the progress of their process.
Faculty
Advisors have access to the esentials.
Will have access to List of students in need of advisement. Able to view their advisement form, give the student advising notes, and approve or reject the form.
Faculty can edit the Advisement Form & Workflow
The staff can also edit the Computer Science Curriculum (live advisement form). The staff can also edit and save the workflow. They can select what permissions are needed depending on credits and grades change.
Advisors are here to help students!
Academic advisors assist students in class planning, major and minor selections, career and internship options, graduate school opportunities, and research possibilities. In addition, they work with every department on campus to bring resources to students.
Advising made easy!
Advisors/Faculty can see the student’s info and view the student’s advisement form. In this form, the adviser can see the student’s live advisement form and write their advising notes. They can also approve or decline the student’s form and submit their decision.
We are an Online Advisement Platform
We are here with a purpose, to make online advisement easier for both students and faculty. In hopes of combating the low graduation and college drop out rates. AdviseMe serves as a one-stop-shop for tracking degree completion, creating a personalized degree plan, and simplifying the advisement process for both students and advisors at CCNY.
